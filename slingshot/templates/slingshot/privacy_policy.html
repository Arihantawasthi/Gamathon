Privacy Policy:

OVERVIEW :

Gamathon Pvt Ltd ("Gamathon", "our", "us" or "we") provides this Privacy Policy to inform you of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal and other information that we receive or obtain from visitors to www.gamathon.gg and/or other websites and property managed by Gamathon or our affiliates ("Gamathon Sites"), and from customers and other users of the services and offerings of Gamathon and its affiliates (the "Services").


We develops, operates and maintains software that both helps organizers create and manage competitions online video game tournaments and helps gamers find tournaments to join. We update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will provide you with reasonable notice of any material changes before the changes take effect by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Gamathon Sites. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes. The date of the last revision appears at the top of the Privacy Policy. As used in this Privacy Policy, the terms "using" and "processing" information include using cookies on a computer, subjecting the information to statistical or other analysis and using or handling information in any way, including, but not limited to collecting, storing, evaluating, modifying, deleting, using, combining, disclosing and transferring information within our organization or among our affiliates internationally.


We collect, use and disclose information to administer, monitor, and improve the Gamathon Sites, the features and content, to provide and improve the Services and verify that users of the Services meet the criteria required to process their requests. We do this so as to improve user experiences for Gamathon Site visitors, customers, and users of our Services.


By accepting this Privacy Policy, using our Services or providing us with your Personal Information (as defined below), you consent to the collection, use, disclosure (including transfer) and sharing of your Personal Information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Please do not provide us with any Personal Information if you do not agree to us using your Personal Information in this manner.


How We Collect Information; Kinds of Information We Collect :


You consent to us collecting information by using "log data" and "cookies," by obtaining information from your usage of our Services, and by asking for information when you buy a product, register for and/or use a Service, or do various other things, as described below.


Log Data - You agree that when you visit the Gamathon Sites, whether as a non-registered visitor just browsing or as an individual who has registered via the Gamathon Sites (any of these, a "Gamathon User"), our servers may automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your computer’s Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, the webpage you were visiting before you came to a Gamathon Site, pages of the Gamathon Sites that you visit, information you search for on our Gamathon Sites and other statistics.


Cookies- Like many websites, we use "cookies" -- small data files transferred to your computer’s hard disk – to collect information. We use two kinds of cookies. First, we utilize session ID cookies to enable certain features of the Gamathon Sites, to better understand how visitors interact with the Gamathon Sites, to monitor aggregate usage by Gamathon Users, and to monitor web traffic routing on the Gamathon Sites. Second, we utilise persistent cookies for persons who have registered with Gamathon to save their login information for future logins to the Gamathon Sites. While session cookies are deleted from your computer when you log off from the Gamathon Sites and then close your browser, persistent cookies remain. You can set your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions of the Gamathon Sites or all functionality of the Services.


Personal Information We ask for and/or receive Personal Information from you. At times, we ask you for personally identifiable information – information about you that can be used to identify you by itself or together with information which we have or are likely to have access to ("Personal Information") – which includes, but is not limited to, your name, photo, phone number, email address, website URL, instant message contact information, and postal address. In addition, we also obtain Personal Information by collecting and maintaining data on your use of Services, such as information on frequency, time and duration of use, and similar. You do not have to provide your Personal Information, but then certain features, offerings or Services may not be available to you, or you may not be able to complete a transaction. We use your Personal Information mainly to provide the Services, complete your transactions, and verify your identity to administer or respond to your inquiries or prevent fraud, or similar.
